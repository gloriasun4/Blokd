Study Better, Not Harder

Pomodoro Method!

One pomodoro cycle = working hard for 25 minutes, then taking a 5 minute break. After doing 4 pomodoro cycles (about 2 hours), take a longer break of 15 to 30 minutes

 20-20-20 Rule!

Study for 20 minutes, and then look at something that is 20 feet away for 20 seconds. This gives your eyes a break
Getting Things Done Method!

Follow these 5 steps - Capture, Clarify, Organize, Reflect, and Do - to get work done.

 Study group!

Form a study group with friends so you can keep each other accountable!
